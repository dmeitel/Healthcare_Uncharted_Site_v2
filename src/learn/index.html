The Organizing Framework

The 4Ps of Healthcare

Every article on this site maps to one or more of the four actors who shape how healthcare actually works. Understanding the relationships between them is the foundation of clinical informatics.

Why the 4Ps Framework
Healthcare is not a single system — it is four overlapping systems with different incentives, data structures, and definitions of success. A Payer's definition of a "patient" is a claims record. A Provider's definition is a clinical encounter. A Patient's definition is their own life. Policy Makers try to align all three. Every piece of healthcare technology exists at the intersection of these four perspectives — and most failures happen when that intersection is ignored.
Patient — the person receiving care
Provider — clinicians and health systems
Payer — insurers and benefits administrators
Policy Makers — regulators, legislators, standards bodies
